Laptop Hardware Troubleshooting Guide

Common Issues:
- Battery not charging or draining quickly
- Overheating and unexpected shutdowns
- Touchpad, keyboard, or camera not working
- Screen flickering or display issues

Possible Causes:
- Faulty hardware components (charger, battery)
- Outdated or corrupt drivers
- Dust accumulation causing overheating
- Misconfigured display settings

Resolution Steps:
1. Verify battery and charger functionality.
2. Update or reinstall device drivers.
3. Clean vents and internal fans to prevent overheating.
4. Adjust display settings and check refresh rate.
5. Test touchpad, keyboard, and camera functionality.
6. Replace faulty hardware if issues persist.

Tips:
- Schedule regular hardware maintenance.
- Keep system drivers up to date.
